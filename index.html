Who is Iron Man?

Iron Man aka Tony Stark is a person whom with his intelligence and vast knowledge, made his way to the top. He is an ingenious scientist who is also a wealthy American businessman who created a powered armor suit to escape a group of people who tried to kidnap him. Later, he developed the suit and added more weapons and functionality to the suit that enabled him to protect the world. Owner of the Stark Industries, his feats in science are a work of art. Not to mention the fact that he was the one who made navigation through quantum tunneling i.e. time travel a reality. Who can forget his AI(Jarvis) and his Hi-tech lab that just shows miraculous feats of engineering.

Though Iron Man doesn't show it out on the outside, he a guy who cares for his family and the people of the world. Though it looks like he hated his dad, in the Endgame it becomes evident that he loved his Dad and actually missed him. He solely took the responsibility of rescuing the plant at the cost of his life. Overall, an amazing person.
